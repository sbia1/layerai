Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At LayerAI, your privacy is fundamental to how we build and operate our platform. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

The Short Version

We collect only what we need to run your AI assistant. We never sell your data. We never use your customer conversations to train our models. Your data stays yours. Period.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, business name, and billing information. When you use our platform, we process the conversations your AI assistant handles on your behalf — this includes messages from your customers across chat, WhatsApp, voice, and email channels.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve the LayerAI service. Specifically, we use it to power your AI assistant's responses, generate analytics for your dashboard, process payments, and send you service-related communications.

We do not use your business data or customer conversations to train our AI models. Your competitive advantage stays yours.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We use SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure. Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel only and is logged and audited.

Data Retention

We retain your conversation data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently remove all associated data within 30 days. You can export your data at any time from the dashboard.

Third-Party Services

We use carefully selected third-party services for infrastructure (cloud hosting), payments (Stripe), and communication channels (Twilio for WhatsApp, Deepgram and ElevenLabs for voice). Each provider is contractually bound to protect your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data at any time. If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to be forgotten. Contact us at privacy@layerai.com for any requests.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and analytics cookies to understand how our website performs. You can disable non-essential cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out to privacy@layerai.com.
